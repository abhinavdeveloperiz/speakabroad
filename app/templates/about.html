Who We Are

SpeakAbroad is a trusted education consultancy dedicated to helping students turn their dreams of international education into reality. Since 2014, we have supported aspiring learners at every stage of their journey — from selecting the right course and university to exam preparation, documentation guidance, and visa assistance.

Our goal is to help each student achieve their global academic ambitions through expert counselling, personalized support, and a seamless admission process.

With SpeakAbroad, you don’t just apply to universities — you build a clear pathway toward a brighter and borderless future.
